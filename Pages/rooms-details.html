Description

This hotel is situated in the heart of the city. It offers a bar and air-conditioned rooms with free Wi-Fi. Guest rooms are simply furnished and equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a minibar. The suite bathrooms feature a hairdryer and complimentary toiletries. This hotel serves a buffet breakfast each morning in the dining room, or guests can enjoy a continental breakfast in their room upon request. In the evening, guests are invited to enjoy a drink in the hotel’s bar. The reception desk is open 24 hours a day. Additional services available include a dry-cleaning service and a tour desk. Railway station is just a 3-minute walk away and provides direct access to the airport. Couples particularly like the location.
